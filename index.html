Short Drama Video Generation Platform

According to the sentence / script input by the user, it is automatically split into multiple storyboards to generate adjustable characters; according to the character, a consistent first frame of multiple storyboards is generated, and I2V video drive is performed to generate a long video with consistent and expressive characters in multiple storyboards.
